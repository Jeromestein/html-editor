## I. Credit Conversion

### Golden Rule

**1 Academic Year (Full-time) = 30-32 US Semester Credits**

| US Semester Credits | Equivalent Duration |
|---------------------|---------------------|
| 30-32 Credits | 1 Academic Year |
| 15-16 Credits | 1 Semester |
| 120-128 Credits | 4-Year Bachelor's |
| 60-64 Credits | 2-Year Master's |

### Conversion Formulas

| Original System | Conversion Formula |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| ECTS (Europe) | 60 ECTS = 30 US Credits (×0.5) |
| CATS (UK) | 120 CATS = 30 US Credits (×0.25) |
| China | Coefficient = 30 / Total Year Credits |
| Carnegie Unit | 1 Unit = 1 Year Course = 10 Credits |

**Operational Method**:
1. Verify full-time status from transcript
2. Normalize to 30-32 US credits per year
3. Apply coefficient: `30 / Total Original Credits`

## III. Course Level Designation

### Definitions
- **LD (Lower Division)**: Introductory, Freshman/Sophomore level
- **UD (Upper Division)**: Advanced, Junior/Senior level
- **GR (Graduate)**: Graduate-level courses

### 4-Year Program
| Year | Level |
|------|-------|
| Year 1 | LD |
| Year 2 | LD |
| Year 3 | UD |
| Year 4 | UD |

### 3-Year Program (UK, Australia)
| Year | Level |
|------|-------|
| Year 1 | LD |
| Year 2 | UD |
| Year 3 | UD |

### 3-Year Diploma (China Zhuanke)
Generally evaluated as **LD** unless core major courses warrant UD.

## V. GPA Calculation

**Formula**: `Sum(Credit × Grade Points) / Total Credits`

**Rules**:
- Use standard 4.0 scale
- Pass/Fail (P/F) courses: count for credit, excluded from GPA
- Military Training/PE: included unless client specifies otherwise
